UX Requirements Verification

  • ⏳ HomePage starts with hidden scrollbar
  • ⏳ HomePage starts with hidden navigation dots
  • ⏳ HomePage starts with hidden scroll arrow
  • ⏳ Scroll arrow appears after 10 seconds on HomePage
  • ⏳ Navigation dots appear when leaving HomePage

Timer Test

Manual Test Steps

  1. Click "Open Portfolio Site" to open the main site in a new tab
  2. Verify the site starts on HomePage with:
    • No visible scrollbar
    • No navigation dots visible
    • No scroll arrow visible
  3. Wait 10 seconds (or use timer above)
  4. Verify scroll arrow appears with animation
  5. Scroll or navigate to Projects page
  6. Verify navigation dots become visible
  7. Navigate back to HomePage
  8. Verify timer resets and scroll arrow appears again after 10 seconds
